# Squad Scenario Analysis

The verified run used 100,000 simulations, seed `20260612`, the active result
store, and the validated official M73-M104 bracket. It is a sensitivity
analysis, not evidence that squad features improve forecast accuracy.

Portugal ranked fifth in S7 squad strength with 0.968 coverage confidence.
Its title probability changed from 4.195% in the matching base simulation to
4.871% in the squad scenario. Group K outcomes were 48.598% first, 33.307%
second, and 10.955% qualifying as a best third-place team.

Conditional on finishing first, Portugal reached the R16 69.433%, the
quarterfinal 49.372%, semifinal 24.159%, final 13.124%, and won 6.064%.
Conditional on second, the corresponding probabilities were 61.789%,
25.787%, 17.474%, 8.944%, and 4.260%.

The most likely R32 opponents were Croatia 26.215%, Panama 23.795%, England
19.107%, and Ghana 6.828%. Spain appeared as Portugal's R16 opponent in
13.644% of all simulations. The modal final was Spain-Argentina (8.035%),
with Spain the modal conditional winner and overall scenario champion
(21.247%).
